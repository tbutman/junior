About Us

Hello! I'm Junior, the owner and operator of Junior's Moving and Handyman Services. With my reliable van and a passion for helping people, I offer comprehensive moving and handyman services designed to make your transition as smooth and stress-free as possible.

Our Services

1. Moving Services:

  • Item and Furniture Moving: We specialize in moving items and furniture in and out of apartments. Whether you're relocating to a new place or just need to move a few items, we've got you covered.
  • Transportation: We provide safe and efficient transportation for your belongings, ensuring they arrive at their destination intact.

2. Cleaning Services:

  • Full-Service Cleaning: Our team offers thorough cleaning services for apartment move-outs. We ensure your place is spotless and ready for the next tenant.

3. Handyman Services:

  • Repairs and Maintenance: From minor repairs to general maintenance, we handle it all. We fix what needs fixing so you can focus on your move.
  • Painting: Need a fresh coat of paint before your final walkthrough? We offer professional painting services to leave your apartment looking its best.
  • Patching Holes: We patch small holes from hanging art, TVs, and other wall-mounted items, ensuring your walls are in top condition for your landlord's inspection.

Why Choose Us?

  • Reliable and Professional: We take pride in our punctuality, professionalism, and dedication to providing excellent service.
  • Affordable Rates: Our services are competitively priced to fit your budget without compromising on quality.
  • Personalized Service: We understand that every move is unique. We tailor our services to meet your specific needs and preferences.
  • Fully Equipped: With our well-maintained van and necessary tools, we're prepared for any job, big or small.
